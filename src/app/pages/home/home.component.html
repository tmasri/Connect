Welcome to CONNECT! This application was built to make it easier for you to share websites with all your devices, all you have to do is share a website from your phone and it will automatically open here. Here's how you can use it: 1- Download the application on your phone, android link and ios link 2- Login to the app, open your browser on your phone and go to a website you want to share 3- Click the 3 dots on the top of the browser, click share, and click the Conncet app icon And that's it!
